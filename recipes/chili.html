Description

Hearty and filling, this bowl of chili is the perfect meal on a cold winter afternoon. It is filled with kidney beans, onions and diced tomatos, along with warm chili spices to give it an extra kick.

This recipe can be made in so many ways, it is the perfect meal for anyone. For those with a crockpot, just brown your meat, sweat your onions and then toss everything into the crockpot and you will come home to a delicious meal ready to eat. If you are more pressed for time, or, lets face it, just plain forgot to turn it on in the morning, just brown the meat, sweat the onions and add everything to a big pot. Cook for 15 minutes and you have a meal that is ready for the table.

For those that like to experiment, this is a perfect base recipe to work with. You could add bell peppers, hot peppers, different kinds of beans, fire roasted tomatoes, really anything you would like.

Chili Con Carne Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500 grams ground beef *
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 2 cans red kidney beans
  • 2 cans diced tomatos
  • 1 package McCorrmick chili seasoning

Directions

  1. Heat pan over medium heat and brown ground beef, breaking it into small chunks as it cooks.
  2. Remove all but 1 tablespoon of fat left in the pan and then toss in the onions and saute until translucent, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add cans of beans, tomatos, and chili seasoning to a large pot or to your slow cooker
  4. When onions are done, add those and the ground beef to your pot or slowcooker
  5. If using a slow cooker, put the lid on, turn it to low and cook for 6-8 hours
  6. If using a pot, bring all of the ingredients to a simmer, then turn down and let heat for 10 minutes to incorporate the flavors.
  7. Serve with jalapenos, sour cream, corn bread, salad, shredded cheese or nacho chips.

* Don't have ground beef? This dish would be just as delicious using ground lamb or chicken in place of the beef.
