Description

Rich, sweet and full of cinnamon flavor, this Monkey Bread is irresistable. Using canned biscuits, this recipe whips up quick, fast and in a hurry. Don't worry about having leftovers, once you start eating it you won't be able to stop.

Ingredients

  • Canned flaky biscuits
  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cinnamon

Directions

  1. Cut biscuits into small pieces and place half in layer in bundt pan
  2. Warm butter, brown sugar and cinnamon in a pot over medium heat until sugar is fully dissolved
  3. Pour half the sugar mixture over the first layer of biscuits
  4. Add the rest of the biscuits and then pour over the rest of the sugar mixture
  5. Bake in a 180 degree C oven for 30 minutes
